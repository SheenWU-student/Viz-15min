15-min-city in London

“Urban Future” is all about resilience and sustainability. We believe creating a 15-minutes neighbourhood in which residents can get everything they require within minutes by walking, cycling or on public transport will ultimately improve the quality of life. Therefore, 15-minute-city concept is the “urban future” trend.
